About the Movie Terminator: Dark Fate

Sarah Connor has become a true hunter of terminators and is now engaged in the destruction of killer robots from the future. She believed that the main thing was not to let them get to John, but now Dani Ramos has appeared, whose survival also determines the fate of humanity. Alongside her, the mysterious Grace, a hybrid of human and machine, joins the battle.

Humanity paid dearly for technological progress, putting its survival at risk. It is time to put an end to the war between machines and humans. This sci-fi action film is the concluding part of James Cameron's trilogy, rebooting the Terminator franchise.
